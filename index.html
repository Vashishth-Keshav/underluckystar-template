Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are star maps?
    Given a location (e.g. a city), a date and time of the day, we can calculate the exact celestial location of all the stars and constellations in that very moment, using astronomical algorithms. We then project these onto a map and print it for you so that it serves as a memento of your special moment. For example, first dates, wedding days, birthdays or passed loved ones.
  • love these, how much does it cost?
    Depends on the size and the frame. Our smallest print (12 x 16 inches or 30 x 40 cm) starts at $59, shipping included. The product is the highest quality smooth art print on 240 GSM papers. We are very proud of it.
  • How long is the shipping?
    To the US, the EU and Australia we estimate 7-10 calendar days, to Latin America 14-31 days, and to the rest of the world 10-21 days. If that's too slow for you, Express shipping is available to most countries.
  • Do you ship to India?
    Yes, absolutely.
  • How do I pay?
    We accept major credit cards and PayPal.

    You can design, preview and order your beautiful print on our website.
